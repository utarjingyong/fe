    Indian Cuisine

    Chapati

    Chapati is a traditional Indian flatbread made from whole wheat flour, water, and salt. Rolled into thin discs and cooked on a hot griddle, it is a staple in Indian households, often served with curries, vegetables, and dals. Its simplicity and versatility make it a beloved part of Indian cuisine.

    Butternut Squash Curry

    This simple curry starts with onion, garlic, and ginger as the aromatic base, followed by curry powder, cumin, diced tomatoes, and water. The butternut squash is simmered until tender, then coconut milk adds a creamy texture. Fresh spinach is added at the end for color and texture. Easy, vibrant, and delicious!

    Masala Chai

    Masala chai is a creamy, spiced tea that’s most popular in India but is increasingly enjoyed around the world. The primary difference between chai and masala chai is that masala chai includes spices, while chai is simply tea and milk.

    Palak Chicken

    Palak chicken is a popular Punjabi chicken dish renowned for its rich and aromatic flavors. The dish is incredibly fragrant, thanks to the use of freshly ground spices, and it is both healthy and nutritious due to a robust spinach sauce.

    Indian-spiced Chicken Traybake

    Indian-spiced Chicken Traybake is a flavorful and easy-to-make dish where chicken is seasoned with aromatic Indian spices, then roasted with vegetables in a single tray. This recipe combines convenience with bold, vibrant flavors for a satisfying meal.

    Indian Coconut Chicken Curry

    Flavorful and easy south indian style Coconut Chicken Curry made with boneless chicken thighs, ginger, garlic, aromatic spices and creamy coconut milk.
