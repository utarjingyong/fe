Fried Rice

Fried Rice is a classic Chinese dish known for its savory flavor and satisfying texture. This versatile dish features cooked rice stir-fried with a combination of ingredients such as vegetables, eggs, and your choice of protein, such as chicken, shrimp, or pork. The rice is seasoned with soy sauce and often enhanced with aromatics like garlic and ginger, making it a flavorful and hearty meal. Fried Rice is celebrated for its ability to incorporate a variety of ingredients and its quick cooking time, making it a popular choice for a delicious and convenient meal in Chinese cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold or day-old)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shrimp, or pork (diced or sliced)
  • 2-3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2-3 green onions, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic, diced carrots, and frozen peas. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet. Pour the beaten eggs into the empty side and scramble until fully cooked. Mix the eggs with the vegetables.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet. Break up any clumps and stir to combine with the vegetables and eggs.
  5. Add the cooked chicken, shrimp, or pork to the skillet. Stir to mix everything together and heat through.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using). Stir well to ensure even seasoning. Drizzle the sesame oil over the top and mix.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir in the sliced green onions and garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley if desired.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade Fried Rice!
