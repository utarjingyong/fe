Sweet and Sour Pork

Sweet and Sour Pork is a classic Chinese dish celebrated for its vibrant flavors and crispy texture. Tender pieces of pork are coated in a light batter, deep-fried until golden, and then tossed in a tangy-sweet sauce made from vinegar, sugar, and ketchup. This iconic dish is often served with a colorful mix of bell peppers, onions, and pineapple, providing a delightful contrast of flavors and textures. Sweet and Sour Pork is a popular favorite in Chinese cuisine for its deliciously balanced taste and satisfying crunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) pork tenderloin or shoulder, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks (canned or fresh)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (for thickening)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, minced
  • Green onions, for garnish
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Procedure

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and cornstarch. Add the pork pieces and toss to coat. Dip the coated pork into the beaten egg, allowing any excess to drip off.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Fry the pork pieces in batches until they are golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per batch. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the rice vinegar, ketchup, soy sauce, and sugar. Set aside.
  4. In the same skillet or wok, add a little more oil if needed. Stir-fry the garlic and ginger until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  5. Add the diced bell peppers and onion to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  6. Add the pineapple chunks and the prepared sauce mixture. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.
  7. Add the fried pork pieces back to the skillet and toss to coat evenly with the sauce and vegetables. Stir in the cornstarch mixture to thicken the sauce if needed.
  8. Garnish with sliced green onions and serve hot with steamed rice.
  9. Enjoy your delicious Sweet and Sour Pork!
