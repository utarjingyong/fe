Satay

Satay is a popular Southeast Asian dish of skewered and grilled meat, typically served with a delicious peanut sauce. The meat, often chicken, beef, or lamb, is marinated in a flavorful blend of spices before being grilled over an open flame, giving it a smoky and savory taste.

Watch the Video Tutorial

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, beef, or lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water
  • Peanut sauce for dipping

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, combine the oil, soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, garlic, ground coriander, cumin, and turmeric. Mix well to create the marinade.
  2. Add the meat pieces to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Cover and marinate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.
  3. Thread the marinated meat onto the soaked bamboo skewers.
  4. Grill the skewers over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until the meat is fully cooked and slightly charred, about 10-15 minutes.
  5. Serve the satay hot with peanut sauce for dipping.
